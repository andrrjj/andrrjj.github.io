KOŽNA BRESKVA

Kožna Breskva (Leather Peach) is a mix series that I started in 2018. I had two goals in mind with these mixes. Firstly, I wanted to to create an archive for myself to track the development of my mixing and mixing techniques. Secondly, I am using these mixes to hone into and refine a specific style and direction in my musical selection. So, in terms of more functional (electronic) dance music, this is what moves me, and what I hope will move you too.
