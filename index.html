HELLO WORK BYE

From Serbia but born and raised in Botswana, I studied sound and music computing and interactive media arts at NYU between Abu Dhabi, New York and Paris. My work includes sound design for theatre, electroacoustic compositions for ensembles and individual performers, interactive installations and audiovisual performances integrating video, light, and physical computing. I used to play in a marimba band and this is my resume.
